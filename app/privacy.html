PRIVACY POLICY

We recognize that your privacy is very important and take it seriously.

Our Syndica Privacy Policy (“Privacy Policy”) describes our policies and procedures on the collection, use, disclosure, and sharing of your information when you use Syndica and the Syndica platform (collectively “Syndica”). We will not use or share your information with anyone except as described in this Privacy Policy. This Privacy Policy applies to activities by Syndica.

The Syndica platform has two main categories of users:

  • Companies, such as startups or mid-size businesses (“Company” or “Companies”)
  • Individuals, such as MBAs or other professionals (“Individual User” or “Individual Users”)

We will make distinctions between the two user categories in this Privacy Policy as necessary.

THE INFORMATION WE COLLECT

We collect information directly from individuals and automatically through the Syndica Platform.

Account, Profile and Content Information: When you create an account and profile on the Syndica Platform, we collect your name, contact information, and any other information you provide. Your name, photo, and any other information that you choose to add to your profile may be available for viewing to users of the Syndica Platform.

Confidentiality of Sensitive Information: Not all information on the Syndica platform is available for viewing; please note the following scenarios:

  • A Company is able to view the profile of another Company on the Syndica platform, but is NOT able to view the discussions (i.e. the question, user responses, etc) of another Company.
  • Non-registered users of Syndica (i.e. the general public) does not have access to the discussions or any other confidential information of the Syndica platform.

In other words, a Company on the Syndica platform has full access to its own profile and discussions, but has limited access to information from another Company. Additionally, non-registered users of Syndica (i.e. the general public) are able to browse the platform on a very limited basis (they cannot view confidential information such as discussions, questions, responses, etc.)

Individual Users on the Syndica platform have full access to all Company information and all Individual Users’ comments and responses. Additionally, Companies on the Syndica platform are able to view the profiles of all Individual Users.

Communications: When you communicate with us (via email, phone, through the Syndica Platform or otherwise), we may maintain a record of your communication.

Automatically Collected Information About Your Activity: We use cookies, log files, pixel tags, local storage objects, and other tracking technologies to automatically collect information about your activities, such as your searches, page views, date and time of your visit, and other information about your use of Syndica. We also collect and may store information that your computer or mobile device provides to us in connection with your use of Syndica such as your browser type, type of computer or mobile device, browser language, IP address, mobile carrier, unique device identifier, location, and requested and referring URLs. We also receive information when you view content on or otherwise interact with Syndica, even if you have not created an account. For more information, see the Cookies, Pixels and Tracking section below.

HOW WE USE YOUR INFORMATION

We do not sell your personally identifying information – such as your name and contact information – to third parties to use for their own marketing purposes.

Syndica uses the information we collect:

  • To provide you the services we offer on Syndica, communicate with you about your use of the Syndica Platform, respond to your inquiries, provide troubleshooting, and for other customer service purposes.
  • To tailor the content and information that we may send or display to you in the Syndica Platform, to offer personalized help and instructions, and to otherwise personalize your experiences while using Syndica.
  • To better understand how users access and use the Syndica Platform, both on an aggregated and individualized basis, and for other research and analytical purposes.
  • To evaluate and improve the Syndica Platform, and to develop new features and services.
  • To comply with legal obligations, as part of our general business operations, and for other business administration purposes.

Where we believe necessary to investigate, prevent or take action regarding illegal activities, suspected fraud, situations involving potential threats to the safety of any person or violations of our Terms of Use or this Privacy Policy.

HOW WE SHARE YOUR INFORMATION

We share information as set forth below, and where individuals have otherwise consented:

  • Legally Required. We may disclose your information if we are required to do so by law.
  • Protection of Rights. We may disclose information where we believe it necessary to respond to claims asserted against us or, comply with legal process (e.g., subpoenas or warrants), enforce or administer our agreements and terms, for fraud prevention, risk assessment, investigation, and protect the rights, property or safety of Syndica, its users, or others.
  • Service Providers. We may share your information with third party service providers who use this information to perform services for us, such as payment processors, hosting providers, auditors, advisors, customer service and support providers.
  • Business Transfers. We may disclose or transfer information, including personal data, as part of any merger, sale, and transfer of our assets, acquisition or restructuring of all or part of our business, bankruptcy, or similar event.

COOKIES, PIXELS AND TRACKING

We and our third party providers use cookies, clear GIFs/pixel tags, JavaScript, local storage, log files, and other mechanisms to automatically collect and record information about your usage and browsing activities on the Syndica Platform. We may combine this information with other information we collect about users. Below, we provide a brief summary these activities.

Cookies: These are small files with a unique identifier that are transferred to your browser through our websites. They allow us to remember users who are logged in, to understand how users navigate through and use the Syndica Platform, and to display personalized content.

Pixels, web beacons, clear GIFs: These are tiny graphics with a unique identifier, similar in function to cookies, that we use to track the online movements of users of the Syndica Platform, and to personalize content. We also use these in our emails to let us know when they have been opened or forwarded, so we can gauge the effectiveness of our communications.

Analytics Tools: We may use internal and third party analytics tools, including Google Analytics.

Do-Not-Track Signals: Please note we do not change system behavior within the Syndica Platform in response to browser requests not to be tracked.

HOW WE PROTECT YOUR INFORMATION

The security of your information is important to us. Syndica has implemented safeguards to protect the information we collect. However, no website or Internet transmission is completely secure. We urge you to take steps to keep your personal data safe, such as choosing strong password and keeping it private, as well as logging out of your user account, and closing your web browser when finished using the Syndica Platform.

ACCESS AND AMEND YOUR INFORMATION

You may update or correct your account information at any time by logging in to your account. You may, of course, decline to submit information through the Syndica Platform as well.

Your Content. You may edit or delete the comments that you post at any time. When you make edits to Your Content, other users may be able to see the history of those edits in your profile activity.

Emails and Communications. When you join Syndica by signing up for an account or creating a profile, please note that you cannot opt out of receiving certain administrative or legal notices from us.

Credentials. You can change your credentials that are displayed in your profile at any time.

Deleted or Deactivated Account. If you choose Delete Your Account in your privacy settings, then all of Your Content will be removed from public visibility on the Syndica Platform, and it may not be restored by us, even if you change your mind. If you choose Deactivate Your Account, then you will no longer receive any communications from us, and users will not be able to interact with you; however Your Content will remain on the Syndica Platform. Once you deactivate your account, you can reactivate it any time by choosing to log in.

CHILDREN’S PRIVACY

We do not knowingly collect or solicit personal information from anyone under the age of 13 (or under the age of 14 for anyone living in Spain or South Korea) or knowingly allow such persons to register. If we become aware that we have collected personal information from a child under the relevant age without parental consent, we take steps to delete that information.
